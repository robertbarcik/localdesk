# Laptop Replacement Process

**Article ID:** KB-006
**Category:** Hardware
**Last Updated:** February 2026

## When Are You Eligible for a Replacement?

Laptop replacements are provided in the following cases:
- **End of lifecycle:** Standard refresh cycle is every 3 years. You will be notified by IT 30 days before your device is due for replacement.
- **Hardware failure:** If your laptop has a hardware defect that cannot be cost-effectively repaired (e.g., motherboard failure, screen damage, battery not holding charge).
- **Performance issues:** If your role requires software that your current hardware cannot run adequately (must be justified and approved by your manager).
- **Damage or loss:** Accidental damage or theft (a police report is required for theft).

## How to Request a Replacement

1. Submit a ticket through the IT service desk with:
   - Your employee ID
   - Current laptop model and asset tag (found on the sticker on the bottom of your laptop)
   - Reason for replacement
   - Manager's name (for approval routing)
2. IT will assess your request and current device.
3. If approved, a replacement will be prepared within **5-7 business days** (subject to stock availability).
4. Standard replacement models:
   - **General use:** Lenovo ThinkPad L14 Gen 5 (i5, 16 GB RAM, 256 GB SSD)
   - **Power users / developers:** Lenovo ThinkPad T14s Gen 5 (i7, 32 GB RAM, 512 GB SSD)
   - **Creative / design:** Apple MacBook Pro 14" M3 Pro (18 GB RAM, 512 GB SSD)

## Data Migration

- **Before your swap appointment:** Back up any local files to your OneDrive or the network drive (H: drive). IT will NOT migrate local files for you.
- Desktop, Documents, and Downloads folders are **not** synced by default. Move important files to OneDrive.
- Your email, Teams chats, and cloud-synced files will be available on the new device automatically.
- Bookmarks: sync your browser bookmarks to your company account before the swap.

## Returning Your Old Device

- You must return your old laptop at the swap appointment. Do not keep old company hardware.
- IT will securely wipe the device. You do not need to wipe it yourself.
- Peripherals (charger, dock, monitor) are yours to keep unless IT requests them back.

## When to Escalate
If your laptop is critically damaged and you cannot work (e.g., won't boot), call the IT hotline for an emergency loaner device. Loaners are available same-day for Gold tier customers and within 1 business day for Silver/Bronze.
