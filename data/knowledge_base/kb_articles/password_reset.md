# Password Reset Procedures

**Article ID:** KB-002
**Category:** Access
**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Self-Service Password Reset

### Prerequisites
- You must have previously registered your mobile phone number or personal email for password recovery.
- Self-service reset is available 24/7 at `https://password.localdesk-services.eu`.

### Steps
1. Navigate to the self-service portal: `https://password.localdesk-services.eu`.
2. Click **"Forgot Password"** and enter your company email address.
3. Choose your verification method (SMS code or personal email).
4. Enter the verification code received.
5. Set a new password meeting the complexity requirements (see below).
6. Log in with your new password. You may need to update it on your phone/tablet separately.

## Password Complexity Requirements
- Minimum 12 characters
- At least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one digit, and one special character
- Cannot reuse any of your last 10 passwords
- Cannot contain your name, username, or common dictionary words
- Passwords expire every 90 days — you will receive reminders at 14, 7, and 1 day before expiry

## Account Lockout
- After 5 failed login attempts, your account is locked for 30 minutes.
- If you are locked out and cannot wait, contact the service desk to unlock your account manually. You will need to verify your identity (employee ID + manager's name).

## Service Account and Shared Mailbox Passwords
- Service account passwords are managed by IT. Submit a request through the service desk with manager approval.
- Shared mailbox passwords cannot be reset by individual users. Contact the mailbox owner or IT.

## When to Contact IT
- If you cannot access the self-service portal
- If you do not have a recovery method registered
- If your account is locked and you need immediate access
- If you suspect your account has been compromised (report immediately — do not attempt to reset yourself)
