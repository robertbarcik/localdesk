# New Employee IT Setup Guide

**Article ID:** KB-009
**Category:** Access
**Last Updated:** February 2026

## Welcome to LocalDesk IT Services!

This guide will help you get up and running with all the IT tools and access you need on your first day.

## What You'll Receive on Day 1

Your manager or IT will provide:
- A company laptop (pre-configured with standard software)
- Your login credentials (temporary password — you'll be prompted to change it)
- Your employee badge for building access

## First Steps (In Order)

### Step 1: Log In and Change Your Password
1. Power on your laptop and log in with the temporary credentials provided.
2. You will be prompted to set a new password. Requirements: minimum 12 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character.
3. Your new password applies to all company services (email, VPN, intranet, etc.).

### Step 2: Set Up MFA
1. Install Microsoft Authenticator on your personal phone (see KB-007 for detailed instructions).
2. Go to `https://aka.ms/mysecurityinfo` and register your authenticator.
3. Register a backup MFA method (e.g., SMS to your mobile number).

### Step 3: Configure Email
1. Open Outlook — it should auto-configure with your company email.
2. Set up your email signature using the corporate template (Intranet → Resources → Email Signature Generator).
3. Install Outlook on your phone if you want mobile email access.

### Step 4: Install VPN (For Remote Work)
1. Open the **Company Software Center** and install **FortiClient VPN**.
2. The VPN profile will auto-configure. Gateway: `vpn.localdesk-services.eu`.
3. Test the VPN connection while still in the office to ensure it works.

### Step 5: Map Network Drives
Follow the instructions in KB-008 to map your personal drive (H:) and department shared drive (P:).

### Step 6: Access Key Applications
- **Microsoft Teams:** Should be pre-installed. Sign in with your company account.
- **SharePoint/Intranet:** Access at `https://intranet.localdesk-services.eu`.
- **IT Self-Service Portal:** Bookmark `https://itsm.localdesk-services.eu` for future IT requests.

## Within Your First Week

- Complete the **IT Security Awareness Training** (assigned in your Learning Portal — mandatory within 7 days).
- Review the **IT Usage Policy** (see company_policy.md).
- Request any additional software you need through the IT self-service portal (see KB-004).
- If you need access to specific systems or folders beyond standard access, submit a request with your manager's approval.

## Common First-Day Issues

- **"My account is locked":** Likely too many password attempts. Wait 30 minutes or call IT to unlock.
- **"I can't access [specific application]":** Your access may not be fully provisioned. Allow up to 4 hours for all systems to sync. If still an issue, contact IT with the specific application name.
- **"My laptop won't connect to WiFi":** Ensure you're connecting to the corporate SSID `LocalDesk-Corp` (not `LocalDesk-Guest`). Use your company credentials to authenticate.
